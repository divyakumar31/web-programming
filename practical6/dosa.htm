Dosa

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions

  1. Rinse rice and urad dal separately, then soak them in water for 4-6 hours.
  2. Drain the water and grind rice and urad dal separately to a fine paste using a little water.
  3. Mix both the batters together, add salt, and mix well. The batter should be of pouring consistency.
  4. Cover and let the batter ferment overnight or for 8-10 hours in a warm place.
  5. Heat a non-stick pan or a dosa tawa and grease it lightly with oil or ghee.
  6. Pour a ladleful of batter onto the pan and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin dosa.
  7. Cook until the edges start to lift and the bottom turns golden brown.
  8. Flip the dosa and cook for another minute until the other side is cooked.
  9. Repeat the process with the remaining batter.
  10. Serve hot dosas with chutney, sambar, or your favorite side dish.

Enjoy your delicious homemade dosas!
