Pav Bhaji

Ingredients

  • 4 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (such as peas, carrots, cauliflower), boiled and mashed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 capsicum, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons pav bhaji masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Butter for cooking
  • Pav (bread rolls)

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan and add minced garlic and ginger. Saut√© for a minute.
  2. Add chopped onions and capsicum. Cook until they turn soft and translucent.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they are mushy.
  4. Add pav bhaji masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Add mashed potatoes and mixed vegetables. Mix everything thoroughly.
  6. Mash the mixture using a potato masher or the back of a spoon. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. If the mixture is too thick, add some water to adjust the consistency.
  8. Toast pav (bread rolls) with butter on a griddle until golden brown.
  9. Serve hot bhaji with buttered pav, chopped onions, and a lemon wedge.

Enjoy your delicious homemade pav bhaji!
