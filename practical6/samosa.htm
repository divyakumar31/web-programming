Samosa

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water for kneading
  • 2 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1/2 cup peas (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Prepare the dough by mixing flour, oil, salt, and water. Knead until smooth, then cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add ginger-garlic paste and green chili.
  3. Add mashed potatoes, peas, garam masala, and salt. Cook for 2-3 minutes, then remove from heat and let it cool.
  4. Divide the dough into small balls. Roll each ball into a thin circle.
  5. Cut the circle in half. Take one half and form a cone, sealing the edges with water.
  6. Fill the cone with potato mixture and seal the open side using water.
  7. Heat oil for frying. Fry the samosas until golden brown and crispy.
  8. Serve hot with chutney or sauce of your choice.

Enjoy your delicious homemade samosas!
